Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PaperGen

How can I generate a question paper?

Generating a question paper with PaperGen is simple. First, log in to your account, select the subject and topic, choose the question types you want to include, set the difficulty level, and customize the format. Once you're satisfied with your selections, click "Generate" and your paper will be ready to download in your preferred format.

Can I customize the paper format?

Yes, PaperGen offers extensive customization options. You can choose from various templates, add your institution's logo and details, customize headers and footers, adjust spacing and margins, and even change fonts. Premium users can create and save their own templates for future use.

How do I add my own questions to the database?

To add your own questions, navigate to the "Question Bank" section in your dashboard. Click on "Add New Question," select the subject and topic, choose the question type, and enter your question along with the answer (if applicable). You can also add tags to make your questions easier to find later. Once saved, your questions will be available when generating papers.

Can I generate answer keys for my question papers?

Yes, PaperGen automatically generates answer keys for all question papers. When generating your paper, simply check the "Include Answer Key" option. You can choose to have the answer key as a separate document or appended to the end of your question paper. For essay-type questions, you can include model answers or marking schemes.

Is there a limit to how many papers I can generate?

The number of papers you can generate depends on your subscription plan. Free users can generate up to 5 papers per month. Basic subscribers can create up to 30 papers monthly, while Premium users have unlimited paper generation. All generated papers are saved in your account for future reference and can be edited or regenerated at any time.
